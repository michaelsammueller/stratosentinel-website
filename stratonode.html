System Overview

Distributed Defense Architecture

StratoNodes form an interconnected network that validates GNSS integrity through distributed consensus, providing threat detection capabilities impossible with standalone systems.

01

RF Analysis Engine

Proprietary algorithms analyze RF spectrum characteristics to identify spoofing, jamming, and other interference attempts in real-time with sub-second detection latency.

02

Network Consensus

Cross-validates positioning data across multiple nodes to detect localized attacks, achieving detection rates impossible with isolated systems.

03

Multi-Constellation

Simultaneous tracking of GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou constellations for maximum redundancy and global coverage.

04

Rapid Detection

Lightning-fast threat identification with sub-second detection, enabling immediate response to emerging threats.

05

Modular Design

Scalable architecture allows expansion from pilot deployments to enterprise-wide networks without system redesign or downtime, supporting single-station, dual-station, or multi-station deployments.

06

Autonomous Operation

Self-healing network with automatic failover. Nodes continue monitoring even if connectivity to StratoCentral is temporarily lost.

Deployment Scenarios

Tactical & Critical Infrastructure Applications

StratoNodes enable continous GNSS monitoring for mission-critical environments across aviation, defense, energy, and maritime sectors.

Aviation & Airports

Deploy across airfields to monitor GNSS integrity for approach procedures, airfield operations, and air traffic systems. Networked coverage delivers continous awareness of signal conditions that support aircraft navigation and precision landing environemnts.

Military & Defense

Establish GNSS situational awareness across bases, training areas, and operational zones. Distributed receivers provide visibility into positioning signal conditions, helping detect disruptions that may affect mission readiness and force protection.

Energy Infrastructure

Monitor GNSS reliability across refineries, power facilities, pipelines, and offshore assets. Gain awareness of signal anomalies that may impact timing-critical operations and industrial safety systems.

Maritime & Ports
