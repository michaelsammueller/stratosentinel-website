SYSTEM OVERVIEW

DISTRIBUTED DEFENSE ARCHITECTURE

StratoNodes form an interconnected network that validates GNSS integrity through distributed consensus, providing threat detection capabilities impossible with standalone systems.

01

RF Analysis Engine

Proprietary algorithms analyze RF spectrum characteristics to identify spoofing and jamming attempts in real-time with sub-second detection latency.

02

Network Consensus

Cross-validates positioning data across multiple nodes to detect localized attacks, achieving detection rates impossible with isolated systems.

03

Multi-Constellation

Simultaneous tracking of GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou constellations for maximum redundancy and global coverage.

04

Rapid Detection

Lightning-fast threat identification with detection latency under 100 milliseconds, enabling immediate response to emerging threats.

05

Modular Design

Scalable architecture allows expansion from pilot deployments to enterprise-wide networks without system redesign or downtime.

06

Autonomous Operation

Self-healing network with automatic failover. Nodes continue monitoring even if connectivity to StratoCentral is temporarily lost.

DEPLOYMENT SCENARIOS

TACTICAL APPLICATIONS

StratoNodes protect mission-critical infrastructure across defense, aviation, energy, and maritime sectors.

AVIATION & AIRPORTS

Deploy across airfields to protect approach procedures, ground operations, and air traffic management. Network coverage ensures continuous monitoring of GNSS integrity for aircraft navigation and precision landing systems.

MILITARY & DEFENSE

Secure military installations, training ranges, and operational areas from GNSS denial attacks. Multi-node networks provide redundancy and early warning capabilities for force protection and mission assurance.

ENERGY INFRASTRUCTURE

Monitor GNSS integrity across refineries, pipelines, power plants, and offshore platforms. Protect time-critical operations and ensure safety systems dependent on precise positioning and timing.

MARITIME & PORTS

Safeguard port operations, vessel traffic management, and autonomous shipping from GNSS interference that could disrupt maritime safety, security, and commerce operations.
