Our Story

About StratoSentinel

From research thesis to critical infrastructure defense. Pioneering GNSS security innovation through advanced technology and tactical excellence.

From Research to Reality

StratoSentinel began as advanced graduate researach in GNSS integrity and threat detection, focused on identifying spoofing and jamming conditions with high confidence. This academic foundation led to the development of StratoNomad, a portable GNSS monitoring device designed to detect abnormal signal behavior in real environments in moving vehicles.

When presented to industry leaders, the technology's potential for protecting critical infrastructure at scale became immediately apparent. The concept evolved from a single receiver into a distributed sensing architecture capable of providing real-time GNSS integrity insight across critical infrastructure.

Evolution to Network-Based GNSS Assurance

By deploying networked GNSS receivers and analyzing their collective signal input, StratoSentinel unlocked capabilities that are not achievable with standalone devices. This marked the transition from StratoNomad to StratoNode for distributed sensing, and to StratoCentral for centralized intelligence and real-time anomaly awareness.

Today, StratoSentinel offers a scalable platform that provides continous GNSS visibility, early anomaly detection, and actionable insight for airports, defense sites, energy networks, and maritime operations.

Mission-Driven Innovation

Our mission is straightforward: ensure continous trust in satellite-based positioning and timing systems that critical infrastructure depends on. As GNSS interference and spoofing activity increases worldwide, we deliver the monitoring intelligence needed to maintain operational awareness and system confidence.

Every hardware design, software module, and analytical process has been built for one goal: enabling mission-critical operations to stay informed, resilient, and prepared in a world where GNSS reliability can no longer be assumed.
