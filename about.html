MISSION BRIEF

From research thesis to critical infrastructure defense. Pioneering GNSS security innovation through advanced technology and tactical excellence.

FROM RESEARCH TO REALITY

StratoSentinel originated from advanced master's research in GNSS security, focusing on anti-spoofing and anti-jamming capabilities. This academic foundation evolved into StratoNomad, a portable GPS receiver with robust threat detection.

When presented to industry leaders, the technology's potential for protecting critical infrastructure at scale became immediately apparent. The vision expanded beyond individual receivers to a comprehensive network-based defense approach.

EVOLUTION TO NETWORK DEFENSE

By deploying intelligent ground stations across facilities and leveraging network consensus algorithms, we achieved threat detection capabilities impossible with standalone systems. This breakthrough gave birth to StratoNode and StratoCentral.

Today, StratoSentinel provides a complete platform for enterprise GNSS security, protecting airports, military installations, energy infrastructure, and maritime facilities from sophisticated positioning warfare.

MISSION-DRIVEN INNOVATION

Our mission is clear: protect critical infrastructure from the escalating threat of GNSS attacks. As spoofing and jamming incidents increase globally, we provide organizations with the defensive tools needed to maintain operational integrity and safety.

Every algorithm, every hardware decision, every line of code serves one purpose—keeping mission-critical systems operational even when facing sophisticated GNSS threats.
